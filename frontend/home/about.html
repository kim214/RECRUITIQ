Our story

Reimagining recruitment for everyone

Reqruit IQ was built to solve a simple problem: too much time spent screening resumes, and too little clarity for job seekers waiting to hear back.

Our mission

Reqruit IQ is an AI-based job matching platform that helps employers shortlist the best candidates by analyzing resumes, transcripts, and certificates against job requirements — automatically.

We believe hiring should be faster, fairer, and more transparent. Employers get data-driven insights to make better decisions. Applicants get a clear view of where they stand in the process.

Built for modern recruitment teams, universities, and growing companies who need to scale their hiring without scaling their workload.
