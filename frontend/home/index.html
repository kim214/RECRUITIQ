How it works

From job post to hire in three steps

A simple workflow designed for busy hiring teams and job seekers who want clarity at every stage.

1

Post or apply

Employers create jobs with required skills and qualifications. Applicants browse openings and submit applications with resumes and certificates.

2

AI analyzes & ranks

Our AI parses documents, extracts skills and experience, then scores each candidate against the job requirements — so you see the best fits first.

3

Shortlist & hire

Move candidates through your Kanban pipeline from applied to interviewed to hired. Track every application in real time from one dashboard.
